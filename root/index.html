About the Toronto Blue Jays

The Toronto Blue Jays are Canada’s Major League Baseball team, known for their passionate fan base, iconic blue uniforms, and unforgettable moments at Rogers Centre.

Founded in 1977, the Jays have captured hearts across the country with playoff thrills, star-studded rosters, and two World Series championships in 1992 and 1993.
